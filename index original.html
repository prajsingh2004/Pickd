Where it all began

Quiet Confidence..
Clear Intent.

In a world competing for attention, we believe in slowing down — listening carefully, thinking deeply, and designing with intention.

Our work is not about being loud. It’s about being remembered. Our approach blends creativity, clarity, and long-term thinking — allowing brands to grow with confidence.

The Thought Behind PICKD

PICKD was born from a simple realization: most brands don’t fail because they lack talent or ambition. They fail because their story is never truly understood.

We began as a small collective of designers, strategists, and thinkers who believed that great brands are built at the intersection of clarity, intention, and emotion.

What started as conversations and late-night explorations slowly became a way of working — one rooted in listening first, designing second, and building with purpose.

Today, PICKD exists to help brands grow with meaning, not noise. To create identities that feel considered, confident, and unmistakably their own.

The Brief

Reinvent the digital presence for a future-tech conglomerate. The goal was to dismantle traditional corporate aesthetics and replace them with a raw, brutalist interface that screams innovation.

The Execution

We approached this project with a "mobile-first, motion-heavy" philosophy. Utilizing WebGL for fluid distortions and a custom physics engine for UI interactions, we created a site that feels alive. The visual language draws heavily from sci-fi telemetry and analog glitch art, resulting in a user experience that is both disorienting and captivating.
